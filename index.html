Facts about Cats

Cats spend about two thirds of their lives sleeping. Cats can run up to 31 mph, which is just over the speed limit on most city streets. Cats can jump about 6 times their own height. Adult cats in the wild don't meow; only kittens do. Humans have 206 bones while cats have 231 bones in their bodies. Cats are nearsighted, but see great in the dark. Cats are believed to be the only mammals who don't taste sweetness. Cats have fewer taste buds than dogs or people. Cats sweat through their paw pads. Cats have sensitive whiskers, similar to human fingertips.
